Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to our most commonly asked questions about FitLife Gym.

Membership Questions

What types of memberships do you offer?

We offer several membership options. Refer to the Join Us page

Can I freeze my membership temporarily?

Yes, members can freeze their membership for up to 3 months per year. A small administrative fee applies. Please provide at least 7 days' notice before the billing date and contact our membership services team to arrange this.

What is your cancellation policy?

To cancel your membership, we require 30 days written notice before your next billing date. You can submit your cancellation request at the front desk or through our online member portal. Any outstanding fees must be paid before cancellation is processed.

Facility & Equipment

What are your operating hours?

Monday-Friday: 5:00 AM - 11:00 PM
Saturday-Sunday: 7:00 AM - 9:00 PM
Holidays: Hours may vary.

Do you have showers and lockers?

Yes, we provide clean showers, changing rooms, and lockers for all members. Please bring your own lock for the lockers, as we don't provide them. Any items left in lockers overnight will be removed by staff.

What types of equipment do you have?

Our gym features state-of-the-art equipment including:

  • Cardio machines (treadmills, ellipticals, bikes, rowers)
  • Free weights and benches
  • Weight machines for all muscle groups
  • Functional training area with kettlebells, medicine balls, TRX, etc.
  • Dedicated stretching and cooldown area
  • Specialized equipment for targeted training

Classes & Training

How do I sign up for classes?

Premium members can book classes through our mobile app, website, or at the front desk. Reservations open 7 days in advance. We recommend booking early as classes can fill up quickly. If you can't attend, please cancel at least 2 hours in advance to allow others to join.

Do you offer personal training?

Yes, we have certified personal trainers available for one-on-one or small group sessions. Personal training packages can be purchased separately from your membership. New members receive a complimentary fitness assessment and consultation with one of our trainers.
